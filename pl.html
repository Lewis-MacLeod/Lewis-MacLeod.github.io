Potato and Leek Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 Leeks, washed & chopped finely
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled & cut into small pieces
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes
  • 1 litre boiling water
  • Salt & pepper to taste


Method
Into strong pan place the leeks, potatoes, stock cubes and boiling water.
Bring to boil then simmer for 40 minutes stirring occasionally.
Once ready serve, season to taste and serve with fresh crusty bread. Then enjoy!! ðŸ˜‹
