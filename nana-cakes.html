Vegan 'Nana Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 2 overripe bananas
  • 65g light soft brown sugar
  • 50g vegetable/sunflower oil
  • 150g plain flour
  • 1 Tsp baking powder
  • 2 Tsp cinnamon


Method
Preheat oven to 180Â°C / Fan 160Â°C / Gas 4
Roughly mash the bananas with the oil in a bowl
Gently fold in the sugar
Combine the dry ingredients and then fold into the banana mix
Line a cupcake tray with cases. This recipe makes 6-8 cupcakes, depending on the size of your bananas.
Spoon the mix into your cases until they're 3/4 full then bake for 25 mins or until a skewer comes out clean. They should be a lovely golden brown colour.
Allow to cool completely on a wire rack and then dig in! ðŸ˜‹
