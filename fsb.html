Full Scottish Breakfast

Ingredients

  • Sausages,
  • Bacon,
  • Black Pudding,
  • Haggis,
  • Eggs
  • Tin of Baked Beans,
  • Mushrooms,
  • Sliced Tomatoes fresh or tinned
  • Hash browns
  • Bread to toast or Rolls
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Brown or tomato sauce if desired


Method
Place choice of sausages, bacon, black pudding, haggis and hash browns onto grill tray and place under grill on a medium heat and cook for approximately 25 minutes turning occasionally until ready.
Alternatively place on oven tray and cook in oven at 180*c for approximately 30 minutes turning occasionally. Place beans and tomatoes into separate microwaveable bowls and heat approximately 2-3 minutes checking appliance guidelines.
Into small frying pan, heat desert spoon oil and cook mushrooms for about 8 minutes. Eggs can be fried in a little oil, poached in boiling water or scrambled by shelling eggs into a microwaveable bowl, add half cup of milk, mix well and cook in microwave for approximately 4-5 minutes (for 4) depending on number of eggs used.
