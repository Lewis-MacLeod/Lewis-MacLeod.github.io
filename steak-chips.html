Steak & Onions with Chips, vegetables and an egg

Ingredients

  • 500grams Fillet Steak sliced thinly
  • Knob butter
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • Quality frozen chips
  • Peas & sweetcorn
  • Egg, if desired
  • Salt and pepper to taste.


Method
Melt butter in a strong frying pan and add chopped onions.
Allow onions to fry gently until almost transparent then add steak turning occasionally and cook as desired.
Serve with good quality oven chips and vegetables and fried egg if desired. Enjoy!! ðŸ˜‹
