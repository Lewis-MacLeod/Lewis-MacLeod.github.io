Chocolate Toffee Shortbread

Ingredients

  • Base:
    • 120 grams margarine or butter
    • 2 Tablespoons granulated sugar
    • 1 Teacup Self raising flour
  • Toffee:
    • Half tin Nestles condensed milk
    • 1 Tablespoon syrup
    • 3 Tablespoons sugar
    • 120 grams margarine or butter
  • Chocolate to cover top:
    • 1 bar cooking chocolate


Method

  • Base:
    • Into baking bowl cream margarine or butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
    • Add the flour and mix well then spread into non stick or greased baking tray measuring approximately 30cm x 22cm
    • Place in pre heated oven at Gas no3 or 170*c for 25 minutes. Allow to cool.
  • Toffee:
    • Melt sugar, margarine or butter, syrup and condensed milk together in a strong pan and bring slowly to boil stirring constantly then simmer for approximately 10 minutes stirring continuously and ensure mixture does not stick.
    • Remove from heat and pour over shortbread. Allow to cool completely.
  • Chocolate to cover top:
    • Break chocolate into pieces and place into heatproof bowl. Place bowl in a pot with shallow amount of boiling water and allow to chocolate to melt slowly ensuring no water gets into the chocolate
