Omelette with Cheese and Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 3 Fresh eggs, shelled and whisked
  • Nob of butter or cooking oil
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Small cup of Cheese, grated
  • Salt & pepper to taste


Method
Into small frying pan, add desert spoon of oil and heat then add the mushrooms.

Cook gently for approximately 8 minutes and place to the side. If using the same pan, clean and then add a nob of butter and melt.

Add 3 whisked eggs to pan along with mushrooms and handful of grated cheese.

Cook on medium heat for a few minutes.

Omelette will start to hold together but will be raw on top.

Either carefully turn omelette to finish cooking or place pan under a hot grill for a few minutes and allow mixture to finish cooking whilst bubbling up and rising under the heat.
