Top Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 140g softened unsalted butter
  • 110g light soft brown sugar
  • 160g overripe banana (approx, 2-3 peeled bananas)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 200g self raising flour
  • 1 Tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • Milk as required


Method
Preheat oven to 180Â°C / Fan 160Â°C / Gas 4
Grease and line a 2lb loaf tin.
Beat together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy,
mix in the banana and beat again.
Pour in the egg a little at a time until fully added.
Mix together the flour, baking powder and salt before folding into your mixture.
Add a splash of milk if necessary for a soft, dropping consistency.
Pour the batter into your loaf tin and bake for 45-50 mins until golden brown and a skewer comes out clean.
Allow to cool completely on a wire rack before taking out of the tin.

Decorate with a sprinkle of icing sugar or simply serve warm, thickly sliced with a doliop of Greek yogurt.
