Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 325 grams Lentils , rinsed with cold water
  • 2 Ham stock cubes dissolved carefully into 900mls boiling water
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 good sized carrots, grated
  • or tinned carrots 160g drained weight mashed
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Tip: A few slices of bacon smoked or unsmoked adds extra flavour ðŸ˜‹


Method
Into strong pan add the ham stock, lentils, onions and carrots and bring to the boil stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat and boil gently for approximately 40 mins stirring occasionally to prevent soup sticking.
Once ready serve, season to taste and serve with fresh crusty bread. Then enjoy!!
