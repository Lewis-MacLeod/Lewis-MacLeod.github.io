Delicious Homemade Tablet

Required utensils:

  • Large strong pot
  • Large wooden spoon
  • Tin tray approx
  • Grease proof paper
  • Saucer or small dish containing cold water
  • Teaspoon
  • Tablespoon

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Granulated white sugar
  • 2oz Lurpak butter ( I use unsalted)
  • 1 cup full fat milk
  • Half tin Nestles condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon Lyonâ€™s syrup
  • Few drops vanilla extract


Method
Put sugar butter and milk into large strong pan under medium heat allowing sugar to dissolve slowly and completely, butter to melt stirring all time until almost at the boil.
Then add half tin nestles condensed milk
or small tin if available (used to get them years ago!)
Bring very slowly to a gentle boil then add 1 tablespoon Lyons syrup whilst stirring all the time.
Continue boil for approx 5-7 mins stirring constantly then test a few drops into saucer of cold water. If drops feel firm remove from heat.
Add few drops of vanilla extract and beat for 3-5 minutes. Mixture should start to thicken. Pour into tin lined with piece grease proof paper (easier to get out) and when cooling mark gently on the top the size of pieces required.
