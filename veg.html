Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces
  • Half small turnip, or 2 parsnips grated
  • 3 vegetable stock cubes
  • 1.5 litres boiling water
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Method
Into strong pan add the boiling water and mix with stock cubes until dissolved.
Add carrots, onions, potatoes and turnip or parsnips and bring to the boil stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat and simmer for approximately 30 minutes stirring occasionally.
