Chicken & Rice Soup

Ingredients


  • 1 Chicken breast roasted and cut into small pieces or, equivalent amount of chicken stripped from left over cooked chicken.
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped.
  • 2 medium sized carrots, grated
  • Small cup dry basmati or long grain rice
  • Chicken stock made by carefully adding boiling water to, 2 chicken stock cubes and stirring to dissolve
  • Salt & pepper to taste


Method

Into large strong pot add chicken stock and boiling water,
chicken breast, onions, carrots, rice and bring to boil.

Reduce heat and simmer gently for approximately 30 minutes
stirring occasionally to ensure soup is not sticking to bottom of pot.

Once ready serve, season to taste and serve with fresh crusty bread. Then enjoy!!
