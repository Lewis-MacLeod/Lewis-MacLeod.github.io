A Chefs Top Five Tips for Beginners

  • One - Wear protective equipment. Oven Gloves, Mitts and Apron | Some may want to wear eye protection.
  • Two - Be aware of which surfaces you place hot pots and pans down on. It will melt, burn or scar depending on the surface.
  • Three - Using Cork matts of reasonable shape and size to place hot pots or pans down onto.
  • Four - Using oil or butter instead of man made alternatives.
  • Five - Using the right utensils for the job.
