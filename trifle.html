Trifle

Ingredients

  • Sponge cake eg Madeira, Swiss roll or ready made trifle sponges
  • 1 Jelly various flavours available eg strawberry, raspberry, orange or lemon & lime
  • Tin fruit cocktail, drained
  • Tin of Ambrosia custard
  • Tub of whipping or double cream
  • Nip or shot glass of Sherry if desired
  • Chocolate flake if desired to decorate. ðŸ˜‹


Method
Into heat resistant large bowl eg pyrex, dissolve jelly with off the boil water.
Add sponge, broken into pieces along with the drained fruit, and Sherry if desired.
Mix, allow to cool and set in the fridge for few hours or overnight.
Pour custard over the base created in the bowl.
Then whip the cream until slightly stiff and spread over top.
Crumble the flake over the top
